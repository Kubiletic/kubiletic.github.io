Got Questions? I've Got Answers.

What if it rains?

I'm always ready to train! I'll send a poll in our WhatsApp group. If 3 or more people are keen to train, the session is on. Otherwise, we'll cancel for that day.

What should I bring to a group class?

A towel, comfortable clothing you can move freely in (suitable for lying on grass/artificial grass), and optionally, grip gloves if you find them helpful. Don't forget a water bottle!

Is the training suitable for absolute beginners?

Absolutely! I always adapt the training when new members join, ensuring exercises are tailored to different levels. Everyone starts somewhere, and you'll be supported every step of the way.
