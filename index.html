Meet Your "Very Personal" Trainer

My philosophy, my journey, and what you can expect.

I'm [Kubi], a self-taught athlete and currently a student at ALO. My journey into fitness led me to calisthenics, and from there, I delved deep into a multitude of disciplines – Pilates, gymnastics, circus arts, tricking, breakdance, yoga, and stretching. This diverse background shapes the unique "Movement" philosophy at Kubiletic.

At Kubiletic, we focus on mastering your own bodyweight. It's about relearning natural movement patterns, getting comfortable with the floor, and building functional strength that translates to everyday life. We address those often-neglected smaller muscles to help you move without pain and feel more connected to your body and surroundings.

I've personally experienced the transformative power of embracing movement. It reshaped not just my body, but my entire outlook on life, allowing me to attract positivity and truly shine. My mission is to share this gift, helping you discover your own strength, confidence, and joy through movement.

Expect a welcoming and supportive atmosphere in all our sessions. We train outdoors, energized by great music and a positive group dynamic. Lessons are flexible, tailored to the group's needs, and I'm always here to answer questions and adapt exercises for any injuries or concerns. This is your space to grow.

Got Questions? I've Got Answers.

What if it rains?

I'm always ready to train! I'll send a poll in our WhatsApp group. If 3 or more people are keen to train, the session is on. Otherwise, we'll cancel for that day.

What should I bring to a group class?

A towel, comfortable clothing you can move freely in (suitable for lying on grass/artificial grass), and optionally, grip gloves if you find them helpful. Don't forget a water bottle!

Is the training suitable for absolute beginners?

Absolutely! I always adapt the training when new members join, ensuring exercises are tailored to different levels. Everyone starts somewhere, and you'll be supported every step of the way.
